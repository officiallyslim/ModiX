About ModiX

ModiX is a powerful Discord bot designed to seamlessly integrate with Roblox games, providing advanced administrative features, real-time logging, and robust moderation tools.

With ModiX, game owners and administrators can manage their Roblox game directly from Discord, granting leaderstats, handling bans, issuing global announcements, triggering in-game events, and much more. The bot ensures efficient game management while maintaining security and ease of use.

Additionally, ModiX includes a ticket system with logging and transcript generation, making moderation and player support more organized.
